Claude forgets. ChatGPT forgets. Every conversation starts from zero. Ortho changes this. We're the first platform that **remembers your engineering decisions**. Not just code, but context. Not just files, but the *why* behind them. Your architecture decisions (ADRs), dependency patterns, past mistakes—they stay with you.

What makes us unique isn't the AI. It's that we built the **engineering memory layer that was always missing**. We let AI actually see your repository the way *you* see it. We give Claude the persistent context it should have had from day one.

We're also the only system that enforces **human approval gates on every workflow**. No surprise code. No hallucinated architecture. Engineers stay in control. AI becomes the fast hands, not the decision maker.

This isn't just a tool. It's a shift from "AI tries to code" to "AI understands engineering."

## 7. Concept & Objective

**The Core Idea:**

Today's AI coding assistants are like brilliant students dropped into a new school every single day. They're smart, but they don't know the place. They hallucinate the rules. They repeat mistakes. And when you ask them to change something important, you never quite trust the result.

Ortho fixes this by building what's been missing: **an engineering brain for AI**.

**What We Do:**

1. **Understand Your Codebase Deeply** — We scan your repository and build a living model of it: every symbol, every call path, every architectural layer. We know your code better than most humans do.

2. **Remember Engineering Decisions** — Your ADRs (Architecture Decision Records), past refactorings, debt patterns—we store them and reference them. When Claude sees a change, it knows the context you've built.

3. **Detect Impact Automatically** — When a developer asks "what breaks if I change this?" we show the real blast radius. No guessing. No "might affect something somewhere."

4. **Orchestrate Smart Workflows** — We execute ASES (AI-Assisted Software Engineering)—a methodology that keeps humans in control. Plan → Architect → Build → Test → Review → Approve. Every step has a human gate.

5. **Optimize Every Token** — We deliver the highest-quality context with the fewest tokens, so Claude can think clearly without token budget waste.

**Why It Matters:**

Software engineering isn't just coding—it's *deciding*. It's understanding systems, weighing trade-offs, remembering why something was done a certain way. Right now, AI coding forgets this. It's like asking a chef to make a meal but not telling them the ingredients are in the pantry, or that you're allergic to shellfish.

Ortho tells AI the whole story. And it keeps humans as the decision makers, where they belong.

## 8. Potential Areas of Application in Industry/Market

**Where Ortho Changes Everything:**

**Enterprise & Regulated Industries**
Financial services, healthcare, and aerospace can't afford hallucinated code or surprise changes. They need auditable workflows and human approval gates. Ortho gives them AI that moves fast but never without oversight.

**Large Distributed Teams**
When your team spans continents and time zones, onboarding to a codebase is brutal. Ortho becomes the "team memory"—a new engineer can ask questions and get answers grounded in your actual architecture, not generic advice.

**Monorepos & Legacy Systems**
Huge codebases with complex interdependencies (the ones that scare managers and developers alike) are where Ortho shines. Understanding impact becomes *fast* and *accurate* instead of "hope nothing breaks."

**Early-Stage Startups**
Startups need to move fast but can't afford mistakes. Ortho lets small teams punch above their weight—one engineer can understand the full system architecture, see exactly what changes affect, and move confidently.

**DevOps & Platform Teams**
Managing shared infrastructure is an art of understanding ripple effects. Ortho's impact analysis becomes the difference between a boring safe deployment and a cascading failure.

**Open Source Projects**
Maintainers are drowning in PRs. Ortho helps them understand contributor changes automatically, suggest improvements, and merge with confidence.
